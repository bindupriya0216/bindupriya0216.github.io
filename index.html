Approach

How I build reliable automation

  1. 01

    Map the risk

    Identify critical flows, business rules, and failure-prone areas before writing tests.

  2. 02

    Design for maintenance

    Use stable selectors, reusable fixtures, clear assertions, and readable test names.

  3. 03

    Make failures useful

    Attach traces, screenshots, videos, and concise reports so issues can be reproduced quickly.

  4. 04

    Keep feedback fast

    Separate smoke, API, and full regression runs so teams get the right signal at the right time.
